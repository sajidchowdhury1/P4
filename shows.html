Top Movie Cast

Foggy Nelson: Elden Henson

Karen Page: Deborah Ann Woll

Kingpin: Vincent D'Onofrio

Bullseye: Wilson Bethel

Punisher: Jon Bernthal

Review

Daredevil was one of the most unexpected shows I enjoyed because I randomly decided to watched it. The concept of being a hero and lawyer was the most fascinating they both conflict each other. Something that a hero does is not legal and sometimes the law is in the right. This show also goes over how law can be bent for the purpose of evil through corruption. The Kingpin uses his brilliance and power to bend those laws to his will. There are also moments of religion too, where the Matt Murdock has to think why he’s this hero business. These moments are impactful moments because it keeps him being a hero and no become the villain and have people dying.

Rating

10/10
