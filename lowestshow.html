Secret Invasion
Must Not Watch:

Secret Invasion was not made with care or love, it was made with money and destruction. They took one of marvelâ€™s beloved characters and ruined him completely. Nick Fury was a great character and he had his charm in his other movies, but when it came to this show, they made him weak, unfunny, and plain out boring. Nick Fury in the movies would come up with clever plans and he would stick out with some of his lines, but in this show they did not provide any of that. The whole concept of earth being invaded by aliens that could disguise themselves as humans was also not that great. They were given any superhero powers that existed, so their powers are not creative either.
