Guardians of The Galaxy Vol. 3
Top Movie Cast

Star Lord: Chris Pratt

Adam Warlock: Will Poulter

High Evolutionary: Chukwudi Iwuji

Gamora: Zoe Saldana

Groot: Vin Diesel

Rocket: Bradley Cooper

Review

Guardians was a great movie with its action packed scenes to some of the best looking action scenes. Marvel has had issues with their CGI for their movies but I could not notice any issues in this movie at all. Some of my favorite parts of the movies are when we get to see rockets past, this let us know his dark upbringings and who he was back then. This all brought a good ending to the movie, because we get to see how he moved on from it. This movie has me excited to see where the guardians are going now after splitting off and Star Lord is back on Earth where he never got to have his childhood. Overall, great story telling and direction from James Gunn.

Rating

9/10
