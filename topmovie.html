Top Rated Movies

One Piece Film Red
Must Watch:

One Piece Film Red is a really good movie that anyone would enjoy. From its action packed scenes to some really good music, it will have a little something for you. The movie also has really great comedic bits that will make you laugh or chuckle. The hero Luffy of the story had some of the best moments and they revealed a little bit of a power up that will be scene in the main canon show later on. It was also great to see Shanks, which you barely get to see in the show. Uta was a great villain and the her songs were some of the best. Overall, This movie should be watched.
